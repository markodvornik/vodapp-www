PRAVNO OBVESTILO

Aplikacija "PITNA VODA" omogoča vpogled v trenutno stanja kakovosti pitne vode in stanje oskrbe v vodovodnem omrežju za izbrana območja v Republiki Sloveniji.

Aplikacija pomaga uporabniku izbrati pravi vodni sistem za njegov vodovodni priključek, nato pa ga preko potisnih obvestil opozarja na vsako spremembo, ki se zgodi v vodovodnem omrežju.

Uporabniki se lahko naročijo na obveščanje o stanju za poljubno število različnih vodnih sistemov.

Aplikacija o uporabnikih ne zbira osebnih podatkov (njihove trenutne lokacije, telefonskih številk, seznam kontaktov), razen e-poštni naslov, ki je v vlogi unikatne identifikacije uporabnika in je nujen za delovanje aplikacije.

Osebni podatek (e-poštni naslov) se pridobi v fazi registracije. Brez registracije in vpisa e-poštnega naslova, uporaba aplikacije ni možna.

Podatki o uporabnikih se posredujejo koncesionarju, ki opravlja javno storitev oskrbe s pitno vodo, in sicer samo tistih uporabnikov, ki so registrirani za območje pod uprabljanjem posameznega koncesionarja.

Uporabnik ima možnost izbrisa vseh podatkov uporabniškega računa z zahtevo na e-poštni naslov info@vodapp.si.

Vsi podatki se hranijo skladno z zakonodajo.


OPOZORILO: informacije v aplikaciji so informativne narave in ne zagotavljajo točnega odraza stanja v sistemu. Za podrobno in točno stanje v omrežju prosimo kontaktirajte koncesionarja.

ENGLISH VERSION

PRIVACY POLICY

The "PITNA VODA" app provides insight into the current quality of drinking water and the state of supply in the water supply network for selected areas in the Republic of Slovenia.

The app helps users choose the right water system for their water connection and then alerts them via push notifications to any changes that occur in the water supply network.

Users can subscribe to notifications about the status of any number of different water systems.

The app does not collect personal data about users (their current location, phone numbers, contact list), except for their email address, which serves as a unique user identifier and is necessary for the app to function. Personal data (email address) is obtained during the registration phase. Without registration and entry of an email address, use of the application is not possible.

User data is forwarded to the concessionaire providing the public drinking water supply service, but only for those users who are registered for the area covered by the individual concessionaire.

Data is stored in accordance with the law.

Users can delete all data from their user accounts by sending a request to info@vodapp.si.


WARNING: The information in the application is for informational purposes only and does not guarantee an accurate reflection of the status of the system. For detailed and accurate information about the status of the network, please contact the concessionaire.
